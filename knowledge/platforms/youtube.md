**Role in the system:** Depth video + search-durable library. The only platform where content appreciates for years (search + suggested). Audiences 2-4. His on-camera + edit skills remove the usual creator bottleneck.

## Format split
- **Longform (8-15 min):** how-to and explainer library (range reconfirmed 2026, creator-reported). Search-first titles ("How to build an AI memory file (so you stop repeating yourself)"). These earn for years.
- **Shorts (up to 3 min since late 2024; 30-45s is still the performance sweet spot):** discovery feeder, repurposed from TikTok masters. REPURPOSING CAVEAT (platform official, as of 2026-07): YouTube's reused-content policy (support.google.com/youtube/answer/1311392) treats content copied "without substantive modifications" as a channel-level monetization violation. Watermark removal alone does NOT clear the bar; always add original commentary/edits (Mitchell's default practice already complies, keep it that way). 2026 creator sources also report AI detection of watermark-removal artifacts (folklore-tier, but aligned with the official policy).
- **Live/streamed (later):** #18 family-consult format could be a live series once subscriber base exists.

## What works
- Chaptered, tightly-edited screen+face how-tos; retention graph is the whole game: cut every second that doesn't earn its place (he knows this from broadcast). First-30s hook criticality reconfirmed 2026.
- Thumbnail + title as a unit: one concrete promise, readable at 120px. Test 2-3 thumbnail concepts per video (reconfirmed 2026).
- First 30 seconds: state the payoff and SHOW the end result, then rewind to steps.
- Description: timestamp chapters (0:00 start, ascending, 3+ chapters of 10s+), links to Substack + repo. Pinned comment: write it to provoke replies, not just park the CTA. Viewer-to-viewer replies are an engagement signal (creator-reported, as of 2026).

## Timing & cadence (verified 2026-07-06, /timing-check)
- Weekly upload day consistency > specific hour; suggested/browse feed dominates subscription-feed traffic (reconfirmed 2026, creator-reported).
- Publish 2-4h before target audience's evening window; weekends fine for how-to content [unverified since 2026-01].
- Note: the widely-repeated "viewer satisfaction now outweighs watch time" claim has no traceable primary source as of 2026-07; do not plan around it until Creator Insider or blog.youtube confirms.
