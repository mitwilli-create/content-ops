# Discord Playbook

last_verified: 2026-01 (baseline from model knowledge at authoring, 2026-07-05; server lists + invite validity unverified)

**Role in the system:** Relationship depth, not reach. Where AI-dev/builder communities actually help each other. Slow-burn surface: months of presence → invitations, collabs, early readers. Audiences 1-2.

## Target servers (verify invite/rules currently valid)
- Anthropic (Claude Developers), OpenAI Developer community
- Latent Space, AI Tinkerers (Seattle chapter esp.), buildspace-adjacent maker servers
- LocalLLaMA-adjacent + agent-framework servers (LangChain, CrewAI etc.) where relevant to a piece
- Substack writers' servers for the newsletter craft itself

## What works
- Being USEFUL in help channels: 15 min/day answering agent/memory/harness questions IS audience-building; his answers are drafts for future content.
- Sharing work in designated show-your-work channels only, framed as "here's what I learned," never "read my post."
- Voice/stage participation: on-camera-comfortable + broadcast background = he should take AMAs and community calls others avoid.

## What dies
- Drive-by link drops (insta-mute), cross-server blast posting, DM funneling.

## Content flywheel
- Every good question he answers 2x in a week → ledger entry (real audience-3/audience-2 confusion is the best story-idea source in the system).
- Every essay gets a "Discord check": which server's recurring question does this answer? Post the genuinely-helpful excerpt there, in that channel's register.

## Own-server decision
- NOT yet. A dead server is negative signal. Revisit at ~500 engaged Substack subs or when reader replies exceed his response capacity. Interim: Substack chat serves the community function.

## Timing
- Timing is presence-based, not clock-based: consistent 15-20 min daily beats sporadic hours. US evening + weekend activity peaks in maker servers.

How to refresh this: T1 for server lists/invites ("<server> discord invite", vendor community pages); presence norms decay slowly. Refresh via /platform-playbook-refresh before the first outreach push on any listed server.
