## Format that works
- 30-60s primary for how-to (2026 sources now name up to 60s as the educational-content ceiling specifically; general virality clips trend 15-30s); 90s+ only when watch-through is proven. Pacing > length: completion rate relative to length is the ranking signal (SEO-blog convergence, as of 2026-06).
- Hook window is the first 2-3 seconds, visual + spoken (updated from 1.5s; ~71% of retention decisions happen by second 3, creator-reported citing TikTok for Business data, as of 2026-06). ("Your electrician can use AI better than most programmers. Here's how.")
- Screen-recording + face bubble for "watch me solve this" content; direct-to-camera for takes.
- Captions always on. Updated mechanism (as of 2026, creator-reported): most viewers now watch WITH sound (~30% muted), but the muted minority is large enough that captions stay mandatory; on-screen text reinforces the one takeaway.
- One idea per video. The essay's 8 points = 8 videos, not 1.
- End with a loop or a "part 2" hook; completions + rewatches outrank likes.
- Check "intro retention" in analytics after every post: 70%+ is strong, under 40% ends distribution (creator-reported thresholds, as of 2026; the metric itself is platform-visible).
