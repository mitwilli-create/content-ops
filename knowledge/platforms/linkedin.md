**Role in the system:** Professional reach + job-market adjacency (feeds career-ops goals). Audiences 2-4. The exec-disconnect and enablement stories are native here.

## Format that works
- 150-300 words. Hook line MUST work truncated (~200 chars before "...see more").
- Structure: one-line hook → line break → 3-6 short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each) → concrete takeaway → soft question.
- Native > links: algorithm suppresses external links in post body. Link in first comment, or post a full-value native excerpt with "full essay on my Substack" as plain text.
- Document posts (PDF carousels) get outsized reach for how-to content ("Skills, MCPs, tools" as a 8-slide carousel).
- Native video rising; 60-90s talking-head clips from YT content repurpose well.

## Timing (BASELINE Jan 2026 — /timing-check before scheduling)
- Tue-Thu, 7:30-9:30am in target-audience timezone. Mitchell targets both coasts: ~8am PT catches 11am ET.
- First 60-90 min of engagement decide distribution: post when he can reply to every comment in the first hour.
- 2-4 posts/week max; more cannibalizes reach.

## Engagement mechanics
- Reply to EVERY comment within the first 2 hours (each reply = engagement event).
- 15 min of thoughtful comments on others' posts before AND after posting warms distribution.
- Tag no one unless they genuinely contributed; tag-spam reads desperate.
